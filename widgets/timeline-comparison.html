The Bridge · My Edge
I understand why digital construction tools matter — because I've worked without them.

Most candidates learn BIM and Procore in classrooms. They understand how the tools work. I understand why they exist. Having coordinated 200+ workers on Indian job sites with minimal digital tools, then learning US BIM-first, Procore-managed workflows at Northeastern and through my co-op — I can speak to field crews and BIM managers equally. When a clash detection report flags an MEP conflict, I know what it costs in the field. When a Procore submittal log goes red, I know what the foreman will say. That translation is my differentiator.
